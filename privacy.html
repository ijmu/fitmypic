Privacy Policy

Image processing

FitMyPic processes selected images in your browser for normal use. Resizing, compression, format conversion, and preview generation are handled with browser features on your device.

FitMyPic does not intentionally upload, store, or inspect the image files you choose for resizing or compression.

Information you may send

If you contact FitMyPic by email, you may choose to send your email address, browser name, page URL, and a description of the issue. Do not send sensitive images unless required for troubleshooting.

Advertising and cookies

This site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show ads, measure ad performance, limit frequency, and help prevent abuse.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings and through Google advertising controls where available.

Third-party services

FitMyPic is hosted by third-party infrastructure providers and may use third-party advertising or measurement services. These services may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, request time, and page URL according to their own policies.
